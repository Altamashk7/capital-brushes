About

Capital brushes was founded in Delhi in 1995. Capital Brushes has been designing, developing and producing the finest industrial brushes and brush products for more than 25 years. The company leads the industry in finding new and innovative ways to use brushes in a wide range of manufacturing, scientific, technological and commercial applications.The company continues to manufacture brushes based on the latest technology to service the requirements of customers. We are distinguished by our ability to custom design and manufacture brushes to fit specific applications. Capital Brushes Inc. is dedicated to meeting customer requirements for standard and custom-designed industrial brushes. We are in bussiness to satisfy our customers by delivering reliable brush products in a timely manner and at a competitive price. We maintain a reputation for high quality products and services by working to continually improve our processes, products and performances and by maintaining good working relationships with customers and suppliers.
